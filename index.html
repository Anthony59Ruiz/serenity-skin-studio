Good to know

Questions, answered

Everything you might be wondering, before your first visit.

Do the treatments hurt, or need downtime?+

Most are comfortable and fit easily into your day. Facials are pure relaxation; microneedling uses a numbing step so it stays gentle, with just a day or two of light redness; and our peels are tailored to keep downtime minimal. We'll walk you through exactly what to expect beforehand.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?+

That's exactly what your complimentary consultation is for. We look at your skin and your goals and build a plan tailored to you — sometimes that means doing less than you expected. Never a one-size-fits-all menu.

When will I see results, and how long do they last?+

It depends on the treatment. Facials give an immediate glow that deepens with regular visits; microneedling results build over a series of sessions as new collagen forms; and peels brighten within days. We'll set clear, honest expectations so you're never guessing.

Is it safe? Who performs the treatments?+

Every treatment is performed by licensed, experienced estheticians in a private, meticulously clean Pasadena studio. Your safety and comfort always come first — and we'll never recommend something that isn't right for you.

What does it cost, and do you offer consultations?+

Your first consultation is complimentary and completely no-pressure. Pricing depends on your personalized plan, and we'll walk you through everything transparently before you commit to anything. No surprises, ever.
